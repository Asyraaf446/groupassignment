The Origin Story

Luméa Cafe is a modern café established in 2026 with the aim of providing a comfortable and relaxing dining experience for customers. The café focuses on serving western cuisine and café-style beverages in an aesthetic and welcoming environment.


The name Luméa represents light, calmness, and freshness. It reflects the café’s concept as a bright and warm space where customers can relax, unwind, and spend quality time with friends and family. The meaning behind the name also symbolizes positive energy and a pleasant experience through every food and beverage served.


Since its establishment, Luméa Café has emphasized quality in both food and beverages. The café offers a variety of western dishes such as pasta, burgers, and grilled chicken. In addition, Luméa Café serves a range of cafe beverages including coffee, matcha drinks, and other popular refreshments. To complement the main menu, the café also provides side dishes such as fries, nuggets, and light snacks.


With its aesthetic interior, friendly customer service, and diverse menu offerings, Luméa Café aims to be a preferred café choice for customers seeking quality food, refreshing beverages, and a pleasant cafe atmosphere.
